Mod Reloader is the ultimate testing tool for modders and players who want full control.

Features:
- Seamless Reload – Instantly reload selected mods, even in multiplayer.
- In-Game Mod Toggle – Enable/disable mods mid-game with advanced filters.
- Publicizer Support – Access internal APIs, including tModLoader, with publicizer integration.
- Main Menu Upgrade – Test faster with UI improvements.
- Exception Menu Upgrade – Copy errors, retry, prettier logs, and jump-to-code.
- UI Integration – Works with CheatSheet, Hero’s Mod, and DragonLens.

And as always, every feature is toggleable and customizable in the menu and config.
